What is etalia ?

etalia is an intelligent platform for scholarly communication. Based on your reference library, etalia builds your text-based fingerprint and let you focus on what matters to you.

How it works ?

Connecting to your library

etalia works in sync with your reference software manager to identify your interests.

Zotero or Mendeley are currently supported.

Building personalized streams

Based on your interests, etalia builds personalized streams of relevant publications and discussions.

Changing interests or exploring a new field? etalia dynamically adapts.

Leveraging multiple tools

etalia text-mining technology is based on state-of-the-art natural language processing and machine learning.

Engaging in collaborative science

etalia lets you connect with other researchers who share your interests.

Stuck in your research? Post your issue and etalia targets experts to look at it.
