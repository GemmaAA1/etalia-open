About

The Story

{{ brand }} was conceived by two scientists at the University of California, San Francisco in 2015, who felt a need for automated literature searches that save time, are personalized, and never miss an important publication from an ever increasing variety of news sources. {{ brand }} came to life as a web application.

A new way of searches

With {{ brand }}, you don’t have to setup keywords for searches or manually browse journal websites and blogs. You also don’t have to maintain automated searches by topics or authors that quickly become outdated. Nor do you need to sort through search results to find the most relevant publications in your fields of interest.

{{ brand }} delivers scientific news that matches your personalized interest, completely automated and ranked by relevance, saving you time and ensuring no relevant paper is missed.

How it works

{{ brand }} checks what you are reading in your Reference Library (e.g. Zotero or Mendeley). {{ brand }} leverages the content of your library using recent advances in natural language processing, machine learning, and statistics to understand your interests. {{ brand }} builds news streams that match your personal interests based on your library text content. Reading a new paper or extending your research interests? {{ brand }} will automatically adapt.

Many tools in one place

With {{ brand }}, you can retrieve new publications from journals, upload them to your library, share them with friends and collaborators via social media, and more.

Discover

{{ brand }} lets you discover trending publications and hot topics in your area of interest and beyond based on the Altmetric score.
