We are two researchers who think that scientific communications and scholarly dialogues can be improved. To go beyond traditional limits, we have launched Etalia (etalia.io).

With Etalia you can receive personalized article recommendations and join scholarly dialogues around subject matters. Specifically, Etalia uses your “text-fingerprint” based on your personal reference library. Currently we are supporting two library managers: Mendeley and Zotero.

Etalia is free and it saves you time. Check it out at etalia.io.

We would also like hearing from you because your feedback matters to improve Etalia. Tell us what you think at contact@etalia.io or come chat with us directly on Etalia.
