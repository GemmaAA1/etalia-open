Hook your reference managers

PaperStream works in sync with your reference software manager and uses the data in your library to give back a stream of recent scientific papers that match your very own interest.

Not using at reference software manager yet? Check-out Zotero or Mendeley. They are awesome tools to organize your library and, they are free.

How does it work ?

PaperStream crawls the web for all recently published papers. Each paper is crunched by our natural language algorithm and individually ranked based on your interest.

Exploring a new field? Changing focus? PaperStream will adapts as you move forward.

Multiple metrics at work

Your stream is curated through multiple layers.

Among others, Altmetric is leveraged to estimate the social impact of scientific publications.
