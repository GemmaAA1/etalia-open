Your personal guide to scientific news

Etalia delivers scientific news that matches your personalized interest, completely automated and ranked by relevance, saving you time and ensuring no relevant paper is missed.

With etalia, you don’t have to setup keywords for searches or manually browse journal websites. Nor do you need to maintain automated searches by topics or authors that become outdated or to sort through search results to find the most relevant publications.

Your Library Your Stream Your Trend

How it works

Etalia fetches what you are reading by connecting to your reference software manager (e.g. Zotero or Mendeley). Etalia leverages the content of your library using recent advances in natural language processing, machine learning, and statistics to understand your interests and to build streams of publications and trending news that match your personal interests.

Reading a new paper or extending your research interests? Etalia will automatically adapt.
