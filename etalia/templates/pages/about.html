How it works

{{ brand }} checks what you are reading in your personal reference library (e.g. Zotero or Mendeley) and - based on the library content - generates a personal text fingerprint (PTF) as a representation of your areas of interests. To understand your interests, {{ brand }} uses recent advances in natural language processing, machine learning, and statistics. By matching your PTF with the text fingerprints of publications and other public texts, {{ brand }} provides you with a personalized news stream and lets you engage in content-driven scholarly exchanges. Reading/publishing a new article or extending your research interests? Etalia will automatically adapt.

Keep up with new publications

With {{ brand }}, you automatically receive a personalized listing of new scientific publications within your areas of interests. You can upload the publications to your personal library, share them with friends and collaborators via social media, as well as dismiss publications that are less relevant to you. Based on your activity, {{ brand }} learns and refines your literature searches.
