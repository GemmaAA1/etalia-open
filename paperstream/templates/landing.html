PaperStream is a free service for students and researchers.

It helps you stay up-to-date in your field by finding recently published articles that match your interest.

No keyword search. No alert to setup. No journal page to browse.
Paperstream leverages what you store in your reference manager to feed your personalized stream.

Connect your reference managers

Build your personalized stream

PaperStream searches the web for recently published papers. Based on your library, it streams you back the recent scientific papers that match your research interest.

Exploring a new field? Changing focus?
PaperStream adapts as you go.
