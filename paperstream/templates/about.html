The Story

PubStream was conceived by two scientists at the University of California, San Francisco in 2015, who felt a need for automated literature searches that save time, are personalized, and never miss an important publication from an ever increasing variety of news sources. PubStream came to life as a web application.

A new way of searches

With PubStream, you don’t have to setup keywords for searches or manually brows journal websites and blogs. You also don’t have to maintain automated searches by topics or authors that quickly become outdated. Nor do you need to sort through search results to find the most relevant publications in your fields of interest.
PubStream delivers scientific news that match your personalized interest, completely automated and ranked by relevance, saving you time and ensuring no relevant paper is missed.

Our Vision

With PubStream, we intend to facilitate this search process, saving researchers time and making sure they are not missing any important findings. By leveraging the content of reference software library with recent advances in natural language processing and machine learning, PubStream builds a scientific news stream that match your personalized interests. Changing your interests? PubStream will automatically adapt.
